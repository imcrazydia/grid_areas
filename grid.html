10 Tips for your fanfic on wattpad

these tips give a baseline of what to do.

1

Your story title

The title is going to be one of your first impressions so it is important
that your title is catchy. It helps ensure that the book will stick in the
readers mind. It also has to be unique

It should reflect on what is inside the book but also has to be quite
intriguing for the reader. This way it will create curiousity for the reader
and want them to read more. And reading more equals succes.

Make sure that it isn't:
+ Too long
+ Gives too much information
+ Looks like you didn't try at all

2

Book cover

It is one of the first impressions, so always make an effort with it. If you can't be asked to
make your own, there is always the BOOK COVER CLUB. Just find
someone there who can make it for you, there is always a talented
person there.

3

Blurb

The blurb is another one that you have to be carefull with. The blurb is
where you first show off your writing skills. So spelling and grammar are
important.

It has to:
+ Be informative, but not tell everything about the story
+ Keep the reader on his/her toes
+ Make the reader want to read more

4

Spelling and grammar

I don't know about you but I hate it when the story has spelling
mistakes. It puts me off reading. I think it's because if there are any
erros, it shows their carelessness.

Do at least the spell check.

Start if sentences sohuld be Capitalized. 'i's should be 'I's. And yeah the
basic but important stuff.

5

Plot line

Have a unique plot. There LOADS of fanfics that are about a girl meeting
the boys then they fall in love, broke up in some sort then they fall back
in love again.


So yeah, keep your originality.

6

Promote it

On wattpad you can join clubs to share your story. Do reads for
reads or just post a link to most clubs there. Also tell your favourite
writers to read it, and once they do and they comment, trust me you will
have confidence in yourself.

7

Chapters should always have something happening

Whether it's to describe the character more or describing a new setting,
something should ALWAYS happen. And it should always lead on to the next chapter.

8

Talk to your readers

Be nice to your readers. Always take time to thank them for supporting
you, this will gain your more fans and dedicated readers.

But don't go overboard with it.

9

Spacing

Your story shouldn't have too much space in between the paragraphs and
no spacing at all is just as worse.

10

Take pride in what you write

I really don't like it when readers out themselves down saying their
stories are 'bad' and that they don't have a chance to win. It makes
me feel really sad.

That is one of the worst things that can happen when you're writing
hating your own story.


So hold your head up and WRITE.
